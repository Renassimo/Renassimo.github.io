About Us

Plum ProExteriors provides a full range of renovation and construction services for all types of houses. We guarantee a superior quality of our work as we use only the best materials combined with outstanding craftsmanship, in order to bring into life your project just the way you want it.

We are a licensed and insured home improvement company successfully working all over Massachusetts and Boston area. Our company unites experienced specialists with wide knowledge in sphere of exteriors. We deeply devote ourselves to every project, and try to make every single detail unique and remarkable. Our customers fully appreciate our professional qualities, energy, and dedication to our projects.
